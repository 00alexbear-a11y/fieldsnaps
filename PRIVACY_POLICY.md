**Last Updated: October 19, 2025**

## Introduction

FieldSnaps ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and related services (collectively, the "Service").

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. By using the Service, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

## Information We Collect

### 1. Personal Information

We may collect personally identifiable information that you provide directly to us, including:

- **Email Address**: For account creation, authentication, and communication
- **User ID**: Automatically generated unique identifier for your account
- **Payment Information**: Processed securely through Stripe (we do not store credit card details)
- **Company/Team Information**: Company name and team member details for collaboration features

### 2. Photo and Video Data

- **Camera Access**: We request camera permissions to capture photos and videos for project documentation
- **Photos and Videos**: Content you capture is stored locally on your device and synced to our secure cloud storage (Replit Object Storage)
- **Photo Metadata**: Including timestamps, geolocation tags, project assignments, tags, and annotations
- **Annotations**: Text, arrows, measurements, and other markings you add to photos

### 3. Project Information

- **Project Details**: Project names, addresses, descriptions, and completion status
- **Geolocation Data**: Latitude and longitude coordinates for project locations (used for Google Maps integration)
- **Task Information**: To-do items, assignments, descriptions, and completion status

### 4. Device Information

We automatically collect certain information about your device, including:

- **Device ID**: For subscription management and app functionality
- **Device Type**: iOS version and model
- **App Usage Data**: Features used, timestamps, and interaction patterns
- **Storage Information**: Disk space availability for photo storage management
- **System Information**: Boot time and file timestamps for sync operations

### 5. Technical Data

- **IP Address**: For security and fraud prevention
- **App Version**: For troubleshooting and update management
- **Crash Reports**: Anonymous error logs to improve app stability
- **Sync Status**: Upload/download status for offline functionality

## Data Storage and Security

### 1. Local Storage
- Photos, videos, and project data are stored locally on your device using IndexedDB
- Local data persists even when offline
- You can clear local storage through Settings

### 2. Cloud Storage
- Media files are securely uploaded to Replit Object Storage
- Database records stored in Replit PostgreSQL (Neon-backed)
- All data transfers use HTTPS/TLS encryption
- Server-side access controls protect your data

### 3. Security Measures
- End-to-end encryption for data in transit
- AES-256 encryption for data at rest (via Capgo OTA updates)
- Secure authentication through Replit Auth and biometric login
- Regular security audits and monitoring
- Automatic backup and disaster recovery systems

### 4. Data Retention
- Active photos and projects: Retained until manually deleted
- Deleted items: Moved to trash for 30 days, then permanently removed
- Account data: Retained while your account is active
- After account deletion: All data permanently removed within 30 days
