## Technical Specifications

### Video Format
- **Resolution**: 1920x1080 (1080p)
- **Frame Rate**: 30 fps
- **Codec**: H.264
- **Audio**: AAC 192kbps
- **File Size**: < 50 MB

### Screen Recording Settings
- **Device**: iPhone 15 Pro (recommended for Dynamic Island)
- **Orientation**: Portrait (9:16 aspect ratio for Scene 1-4)
- **Orientation**: Landscape (16:9 aspect ratio for Scene 5)
- **Screen Recording Tool**: iOS Screen Recording + QuickTime
- **Audio**: Voice-over recorded separately for clarity

### Accessibility
- **Captions**: Closed captions for all voiceover
- **Text Size**: All on-screen text minimum 18pt
- **Contrast Ratio**: WCAG AA compliant (4.5:1 minimum)

## Filming Notes

### Scene 1 - Automatic Clock-In
- **Location**: Actual construction site (get permission)
- **Time**: Early morning (7:00-8:00 AM for authentic lighting)
- **Props**: Hard hat, safety vest, phone in pocket
- **Key Shot**: Close-up of notification on lock screen
- **Alternative**: If no site access, use exterior of commercial building

### Scene 2 - Personal Timecard
- **Location**: Studio or office
- **Lighting**: Natural daylight or soft white LED
- **Screen Recording**: Use actual app with realistic demo data
- **Key Detail**: GPS coordinates must be visible but not obtrusive

### Scene 3 - Location Privacy Screen
- **Focus**: Toggle interaction must be smooth (60fps)
- **Timing**: Hold on text long enough to read (3-4 seconds)
- **Toast Notification**: Must be clearly visible
- **Key Message**: Worker control is primary focus

### Scene 4 - Mileage & Travel
- **PDF Quality**: Must be crisp and readable at 1080p
- **Data**: Use realistic construction site names and distances
- **Highlight**: Circle or highlight travel time breakdown

### Scene 5 - Benefits Summary
- **Animation**: Stagger quadrant reveals (0.2s each)
- **Icons**: Use lucide-react icons matching app UI
- **Timing**: Hold final frame for 3 seconds
- **Call-to-Action**: App Store badge must be visible
